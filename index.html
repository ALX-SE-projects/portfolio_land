About

Our inspiration for creating a weather forecast application comes from a personal need to simplify accessing weather information. Growing up in a place with unpredictable weather, We often found ourselves consulting various sources to plan our day. This led us to realize the complexity and inefficiency of existing solutions. We wanted to build something that combines ease of use with accuracy, leveraging modern technologies to provide real-time weather updates.
